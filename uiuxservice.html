FAQ

Common Questions

Do you do UX research too, or just UI?
I handle both. Every project starts with research — user interviews, competitive analysis, and UX audits — before any visual design begins. This ensures the interface solves real problems.
What tools do you deliver files in?
Primarily Figma, with all assets, components, and design tokens neatly organised for developer handoff. I can also deliver in Adobe XD or Sketch if required.
How long does a typical UI/UX project take?
A mobile app design (10–20 screens) typically takes 3–5 weeks. A full web platform 4–8 weeks depending on complexity. We'll agree on timelines during our kickoff call.
How many revisions are included?
Starter packages include 2 revision rounds. Pro and Enterprise packages include unlimited revisions within the scope. I work until you're completely happy with the result.
