FAQ

Marketing Questions

How quickly will I see results?
Paid ads (Google/Meta) typically show results within 2–4 weeks. SEO is a longer-term investment — you'll see meaningful traction in 3–6 months, with compounding growth beyond that.
What's your minimum monthly budget for ads?
I recommend a minimum ad spend of ₹15,000–₹30,000/month for Google Ads and ₹10,000–₹20,000/month for Meta Ads to generate meaningful data and results. Management fees are separate.
Do you manage social media content too?
Yes. Social media management packages include content planning, copywriting, graphic design, scheduling, and community management across Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn.
How do you measure success?
We agree on KPIs upfront — traffic, leads, ROAS, or revenue — and I provide monthly reports with clear metrics, insights, and recommended actions for continuous improvement.
