FAQ

Web Design Questions

Can I edit the website myself after delivery?
Yes. All websites are built with a CMS (Webflow Editor or WordPress) so you can update text, images, and blog posts without any coding knowledge. I also provide a brief tutorial video.
Do you provide hosting?
I help you choose and set up hosting (Webflow hosting, Hostinger, SiteGround etc.) but the hosting fees are billed directly to you. I'm available to assist with setup and migration.
How many pages are included?
It depends on the package. Starter includes 1 page (landing), Pro includes up to 5 pages, Enterprise is unlimited. Additional pages can always be added at a reasonable rate.
