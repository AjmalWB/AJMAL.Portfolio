FAQ

Graphic Design Questions

Do I own the final logo and brand files?
Yes, absolutely. Upon final payment, you receive full ownership of all files including the editable source files (AI, EPS, SVG, PDF) plus PNG/JPG exports in all sizes.
What formats do you deliver?
I deliver vector files (AI, EPS, SVG), print-ready PDFs, and raster files (PNG transparent background, JPG) in multiple resolutions — suitable for web, print, and social media.
Can you redesign my existing logo?
Yes. Whether it's a full rebrand or a subtle refresh, I can modernise your visual identity while preserving the brand equity you've built.
