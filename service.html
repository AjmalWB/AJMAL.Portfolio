FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a project typically take?
Timelines vary by scope. A landing page takes 1–2 weeks, a full brand identity 2–4 weeks, and a complete website + marketing campaign 4–8 weeks. We'll discuss timelines during our initial call.
Do you work with international clients?
Yes! I work with clients worldwide. Communication is primarily via email, Slack, or Zoom — whatever works best for you.
What tools do you use?
I primarily use Figma for UI/UX and web design, the Adobe Suite (Illustrator, Photoshop) for graphics, Webflow/WordPress for development, and tools like SEMrush, Google Analytics, and Meta Ads Manager for marketing.
Do you offer ongoing support?
Yes! All packages include a post-delivery support period. For ongoing needs, I also offer monthly retainer plans. Get in touch to discuss your specific requirements.
